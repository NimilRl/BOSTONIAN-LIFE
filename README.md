## üìú Data Story Overview

Boston, Massachusetts, is a vibrant city with rich historical significance, diverse cultural offerings, and robust educational and healthcare systems. The **Bostonian Life** project aims to help individuals make informed decisions about living in Boston by exploring key aspects such as **cost of living**, **housing market trends**, **employment opportunities**, **educational quality**, **healthcare services**, and **environmental risks**. 

Our data analysis leverages multiple datasets to provide an accurate and detailed overview of **life in Boston**. For example, **interactive gauges** visualize Boston's **climate** favorability, rated at 75%, while bar charts compare the **cost of living** between Boston and New York, showing Boston as 15% less expensive. The Story also features graphs depicting **homeownership** rates, rental market trends, and neighborhood-specific rent prices, along with visualizations of school enrollment, quality of schools, and extracurricular activities. 

**Economic opportunities** are highlighted through charts illustrating employment by occupation and industry, income growth by neighborhood, and overall job market trends. Satisfaction ratings for various healthcare components, insurance coverage breakdown, and major **healthcare facilities** are also included, alongside maps and data highlighting flood, fire, wind, and heat risks in Boston. This storytelling approach, combined with engaging visualizations, helps to present a multifaceted picture of living in Boston.

## üõ† Tech Stack

We utilized a combination of frameworks and tools to develop and host this project:
- **Frontend Development**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, D3.js for interactive visualizations.
- **Visualization Tools**: D3.js for dynamic charts, Leaflet for interactive maps, and amCharts for additional visual elements.
- **Hosting**: Deployed on GitHub Pages for easy access and collaboration.
- **UI Development**: WordPress for user interface design.
- **Infographics**: Created using Canva for visually appealing graphics.
- **APIs**: Google Maps and Weather APIs for real-time data integration.

## üîç Datasets and References Used

Our analysis leverages multiple datasets to provide an accurate and detailed overview of life in Boston:
- **Cost of Living Data**: Extracted from costofliving.com and Numbeo.
- **Housing Data**: Sourced from neighborhoodscout.com and real estate databases.
- **Economic and Employment Data**: Collected from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics and local government reports.
- **Educational Data**: From local school district reports and national education databases.
- **Healthcare Data**: Gathered from public health databases and hospital reports.
- **Climate Data**: Compiled from weather.com and climate reports.
- **Environmental Risk Data**: From floodfactor.com and other risk assessment tools.
